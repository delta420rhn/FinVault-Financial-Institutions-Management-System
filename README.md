# FinVault-Financial-Institutions-Management-System
A Stock and Trade Management System enabling seamless workflows between Auditor, Stock Manager, Trade Manager, Compliance Officer, and Fraud Analyst. It supports stock tracking, trade processing, audit validation, compliance monitoring, and fraud detection through role-based interfaces.


Stock & Trade Management System
Overview

This project is a Stock and Trade Management System designed to support efficient workflows between Auditor, Stock Manager, Trade Manager, Compliance Officer, and Fraud Analyst. It ensures smooth stock handling, trade execution, auditing, compliance, and fraud detection.

Features
Stock quantity management and reporting
Trade transaction processing (buy/sell)
Audit validation and correction recommendations
Compliance monitoring with regulatory updates
Fraud detection and investigation workflows

System Roles
Auditor: Reviews stock updates, validates transactions, ensures compliance
Stock Manager: Handles inventory, pricing, and stock reports
Trade Manager: Executes trades and manages buy/sell requests
Compliance Officer: Oversees regulatory requirements
Fraud Analyst: Detects and investigates suspicious activities

Purpose

This system demonstrates a structured, role-based approach to managing stock trading operations with transparency, accountability, and security.
